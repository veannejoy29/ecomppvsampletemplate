How to Order

Step 1: Choose Your Product

Browse through our catalog of pots and vases. Click on any item to view its details and price.

Step 2: Contact Us

Send us a message with the product name and your contact information through our email, or phone number provided on the website.

Step 3: Confirm Order

We will get in touch with you to confirm your order details, shipping information, and payment options.

Step 4: Make Payment

Complete your payment via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card as per the instructions provided.

Step 5: Receive Your Order

Once the payment is confirmed, we will ship your order. You will receive a tracking number to monitor the delivery status.

Step 6: Enjoy Your Product

Unpack your beautiful pots and vases and add a touch of elegance to your space. We would love to hear your feedback!
